A distant song, 2018

Sound-performance, light

The public stands at the beginning of a long completely dark hallway. They can't cross a line, that is marked on the ground in front of them. At the end of the hallway there is a opened door, a door to a room. The light of this room is turned on. From a distance, the public listens to a live musical piece, that comes from the room. After the piece, the light is turned off again.
