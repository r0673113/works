The take off, 2018

Performative sculpture, textile and wood

A ten-meter-long white/blue shimmering textile is thrown from a thirty-meter-high window like a sheet. It depends on a pennant, and now it just hangs, dancing in the wind. Then it slowly starts making wave-movements, as if it's moving upwards.
